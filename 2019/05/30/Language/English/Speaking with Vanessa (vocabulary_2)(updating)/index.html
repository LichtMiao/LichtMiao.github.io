Speaking with Vanessa (vocabulary_2)(updating)

投稿日 2019-05-30 | In Language , English

affix词缀，prefix前缀，suffix后缀，复数都是加-es，hyphen连字符

prefix

pre-

pre-: before
preview
prefix

anti-

anti-: against
anticlimatic，climatic气候的；气候上的；由气候引起的；受气候影响的adj.
anti-inflammatory抗炎的，抗压药adj.n.，inflammatory有炎症的，煽动性的，激动的/ɪn’flæmət(ə)rɪ
antiwar反对战争的adj.

non-

non-: not
nonviolent
nonsense胡说，废话，荒谬的n.adj.，sense感觉

in-, im-, il-, ir-

in-, im-, il-, ir-: not
indirect
injustice
immoral不道德的，moral道德的
illiterate，literate识字的，有文学素养的adj.
irreverent不敬的，reverent虔诚的，恭敬的adj.
irresponsible

de-

de-: opposite
devalue使贬值
destruction解析: the opposite of construction

dis-

dis-: not, opposite
discover
disagree
disproportionate/,dɪsprə’pɔːʃ(ə)nət/不成比例的

un-

un-: not, opposite of
unhealthy
unfriendly
unusual

en-, em-

en-, em-: cause to
enact
enable使能够v.，able，be able to能；[经管] 有能力的；能干的adj.
enpower授权，允许，使能够vt.
embrace拥抱，brace支柱，支撑v.n.

fore-

fore-: before, front of
foreshadow预示，预兆vt.n.
forearm
forecast

in-, im-

in-, im-: in
income收入，收益
inpulse脉冲

inter-

inter-: between, among
interrupt
interact相互作用vt.

mid-

mid-: middle
midway
mid-sentence

mis-

mis-: wrongly
misspell
misstep
misdemeanor轻罪；品行不端n.，demeanor风度，举止n.

over-

over-: over, too much
overlook忽视
overeat吃过量v.

re-

re-: again
rewrite
research
review
refresh
refurbish/riː’fɜːbɪʃ/整修，翻新重装；修改，完善v.，furbish磨光，恢复，更新vt.

semi-

semi-: half, partly, not fully
semifinal半决赛的，半决赛阶段
semiformal半正式的

sub-

sub-: under
subway
submarine海底的，海洋生物，潜水艇adj.n.

under-

under-: under, too little
underestimate/ʌndər’estɪmeɪt/低估，estimate评价，估计
under-reported低估，少报v.

super-

super-: above, beyond
superhuman
superstar

trans-

trans-: across a boundary
transmit传输，传播
transport

suffix

-able, -ible

-able, -ible: is, can be
affordable
comfortable
sensible/‘sensɪb(ə)l/明智的，通情达理的

-al, -ial

-al, -ial: having characteristics of
universal
facial

-ed

-ed: past tense, adjective
tense紧张的，拉紧adj.v.，时态n.
The door is opened. The opened door.

-en

-en: made of
golden
wooden

-er, -or

-er, -or: person connected with a job
teacher
worker
professor

-ve, -active, -itive

-ve, -active, -itive: an adjective
active
formative
sensitive

-er

-er: more
taller
stronger
faster
smarter

-est

-est: the most
talleast
strongest
smartest

-ful

-ful: full of
hurtful
helpful
careful
graceful/‘greɪsfʊl; -f(ə)l/优雅的，优美的adj.

-ing

-ing: to create verbs
sleeping
running
crying

-y

-y: characterized by
happy
gloomy/‘gluːmɪ/沮丧的，阴郁的，黑暗的
sappy/‘sæpɪ/精力充沛的；树液多的；愚笨的

-ic

-ic: having characteristic of sth.
poetic/pəʊ’etɪk/诗的，诗歌的；诗意的；诗人的adj.，poem/‘pəʊɪm/诗
linguistic/lɪŋ’gwɪstɪk/语言的，语言学的

-ous, -eous, -ious

-ous, -eous, -ious: having qualities of
joyous/‘dʒɒɪəs/
courageous/kə’reɪdʒəs/有胆量的
gracious/‘greɪʃəs/高兴的，和谐的，雅致的

-ion, -tion, -ation

-ion, -tion, -ation: act, process
occassion
motion动作，手势，提案，议案v.n.
relation
attraction

-ity, -ty

-ity, -ty: state of
activity
society
infinity

-less

-less: without
hopeless
fearless
helpless
loveless

-ness

-ness: state of
openness
kindness
happiness

-ly

-ly: how something is, adverb
lovely
slowly
unfortunately

-ment

-ment: state of being
contentment满意，满足，content内容，目录n.,满意的，使满意adj.vt.
enjoyment
